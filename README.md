## The Vision: Bridge the Gap Between Performance and Profit.

**Lumina** is an open-source, agentic orchestration framework designed for executives and entrepreneurs who refuse to choose between business scale and personal well-being. It is the first operating system that optimizes **both** your sales funnel and your biometric data in real-time.

Traditional automation creates cognitive overload. Lumina uses advanced AI agents to handle the massive complexity of corporate systems, allowing the human "Chief Architect" to focus only on high-leverage decisions during their peak cognitive windows.

### Why We Need Claude Max 20x

Lumina is not a simple script. It requires managing incredibly large and disparate context windows to maintain operational coherence:
1.  **Cross-Domain Orchestration:** Maintaining state while synthesizing medical/biometric data alongside financial compliance legalities.
2.  **Massive Codebase Refactoring:** Automatically refactoring agent interactions as APIs (like Apollo/Ademicon/biometric hardware) evolve.

---

## Architecture

Lumina is structured into core performance modules:

### 1. `/agents/finance` (The Profit Core)
Focuses on the full automation of complex sales cycles, specializing in Brazilian financial products (Consórcios).
* **Apollo Lead Hunter Agent:** Automatically identifies, qualifies, and enriches high-net-worth leads.
* **Funnel Optimization Agent:** Manages the entire sales pipeline, generating personalized proposals and contracts based on real-time Ademicon product data.

### 2. `/agents/bio_performance` (The Cognitive Core)
Focuses on quantifying and optimizing the human-in-the-loop.
* **Bio-Metric Audit Agent:** Analyzes executive data (sleep, HRV, glucose via API integrators like Terra/Vital) to determine readiness.
* **Deep Work Scheduler:** Dynamically locks focus blocks during peak bio-performance windows.
